Intro

Through rigorous projects and assignments, I have gained the practical mastery of Excel, PostgreSQL, Python, and Tableau for data analysis and communication. I learned how to extract and present actionable insights that deliver business value from common data sources such as CSV files and SQL databaes. Check out my work.
